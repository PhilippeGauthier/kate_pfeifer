Fees

My fee for a 50 minute session is $100 and a 90 minute session is $150. Fees are payable either in cash or via check made out to my supervisor, Geoff Nugent.

I provide a free initial phone session prior to scheduling a first visit so that I can get to know a little bit about you, your current situation and your goals for treatment as well as answer any questions you might have about myself, my experience with specific issues or how I might best be able to be of service to you.

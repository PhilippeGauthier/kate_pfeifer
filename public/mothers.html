Expecting & New Moms

Expecting a new child is a time of great joy but also one which presents a myriad of choices which can at times feel overwhelming or frightening. It is possible that issues arise unexpectedly once a woman learns she is going to be a parent, and especially as the birth approaches. It is not uncommon to experience a wide range of emotions during the pregnancy, birth and postpartum period.

This is a particularly sensitive time hormonally and an extremely powerful and life-changing time physically and emotionally. I am passionate about serving expecting and new mothers and helping you on your journey through this very special period.

I have experience and training working with women suffering from perinatal mood disorders, incuding postpartum depression and anxiety. I hope you will consider working with me if you find yourself looking for a supportive, safe place to be.
